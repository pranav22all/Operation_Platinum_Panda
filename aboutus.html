About Us

The Cheng Foundation is run by both Bryce and Darrell Cheng, the foundation presidents. Brief descriptions of each co-president are available below.

Bryce Cheng

Childhood

At an early age, Bryce Cheng was tasked with the heavy burden of upholding the prestigious Cheng Family Legacy. As the oldest of the "twin dragons,"" Bryce was tasked with "making the Cheng Family great again" in the then foreign continent of North America. Alongside his younger brother, Darrell, Bryce underwent integrated training in University of Washington Academy for Young Scholars to acquire the skills that are necessary to survive in the brutal and capitalist world of the United States.

Rice to Greatness

After receiving a "miniscule" loan of two-million dollars from their family fortune, Bryce established a strong foothold in the American economy by investing in construction through his newfound company, The Cheng Organization. Through said company, Bryce monopolized the market for construction and property development (twice running Donald Trump Bankrupt). In addition, Bryce was able to curb the inevitable downsizing through lobbying and bribery of the U.S. government with his now 30-billion dollar net worth.

Enlightenment and Future Investment in Philanthropy

After monopolizing the construction market through aggressive negotiations and heartless actions, Bryce Cheng was now a leading figure in American Business earning himself a position in the Fortune 500. However, Cheng was haunted by the costs of his success in the construction market after witnessing the countless competitors that lay bankrupt in the wake of his rise. In order to cope with the burdensome guilt of witnessing the numerous failures that precipitated into his success, Bryce Cheng, with the help of Darrel Cheng (now CEO of Cheng Consolidated Entertainment Facilities), merged to found the Cheng Foundation.

Darrell Cheng

Childhood

In his childhood, Darrell was constantly neglected by his parents due to being the younger child. This constant neglect during his development prompted Darrell to train intensively in order to one day surpass his brother, Bryce. As the other half of the "Twin Dragons," Darrell went to the University of Washington Academy for Young Scholars in order to gain the skills necessary to survive in the high-stakes business environment.

Rice to Greatness

After graduating from the University of Washing Academy for Young Scholars, Cheng established a strong foothold in the Entertainment Business by constructing a slew of high-end night clubs in government-designated brownfields, lands previously considered "lost" due to excessive human abuse. By doing so, Cheng won the Nobel Peace Prize in 2014 for the effective reclamation of brownfields. In addition, Darrell was able to establish a monopoly in the entertainment business by suing other companies on behalf of contamination of the environment. As a result, the company, "Cheng Consolidated Entertainment Facilities" was born.

Enlightenment and Future Investment in Philanthropy

At the epitome of his success, Cheng was described as an ignorant and inconsiderate playboy. However, this all changed when Darrell's yacht, the Verdant Legacy, capsized during a storm. Cheng later spent five months on the distant island of Lien Choy. Not much was known during his five month excursion, but Darrell proclaimed himself a changed man after he was rescued by Mongolian Fisherman. In order to atone for his past actions as a playboy leach of the Cheng Family, Darrell co-founded the Cheng foundation with his brother, Bryce.
