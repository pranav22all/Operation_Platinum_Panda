Additional Information:

Site intended as a satirical critique on the United States' historical infatuation with wealth and the people who hold it, as well as a critique of the Gospel of Wealth and Social Darwinism.

Site created from scratch using Bootstrap in conjunction with HTML, CSS, and JQuery with an attempted focus on a gray-based old-school (late 2000s) aesthetic.

Undertaken by Pranav Sriram and Darrell Cheng for a 2017 APUSH Project
